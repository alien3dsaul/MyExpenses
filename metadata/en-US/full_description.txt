<p>My Expenses keeps track of your expenses and your income, directly on your smartphone and on your tablet.</p>

<ul>
<li>Up to five accounts with <b>transfers</b> (also between different currencies)</li>
<li>Unlimited number of <b>templates</b> and up to three plans for <b>recurring</b> transactions</li>
<li>Transactions can be <b>grouped</b> (per day, week, month and year) and <b>filtered</b></li>
<li><b>Export</b> to QIF and CSV. <b>Import</b> from QIF</li>
<li>Data can be protected by <b>password</b></li>
<li><b>Theme</b> (dark and light) and <b>font size</b> can be customized</li>
<li><b>Reconciliation</b>: Easily compare transaction status with your bank statements</li>
<li>Homescreen <b>widget</b> and <b>shortcuts</b> for quick data entry</li>
</ul>

<p>Premium features are unlocked by purchase of one of the following licence keys:</p>

<p><b>Contrib Key</b></p>

<ul>
<li><b>No ads</b></li>
<li>Unlimited number of <b>accounts</b></li>
<li>Unlimited number of plans for <b>recurring</b> transactions</li>
<li><b>Pie chart</b>: Visualize the statistical distribution of transactions per category over different periods (years, months, weeks, days)</li>
<li><b>Split transaction</b>: A single transaction can be split into multiple parts (categories and transfers)</li>
<li><b>Print</b> the transaction list to a PDF file</li>
<li>Define a <b>security question</b> for recovering a lost password</li>
<li><b>Image attachment</b>: Capture via camera or select from gallery</li>
<li>Quick <b>template</b> selection from homescreen <b>widget</b></li>
</ul>

<p><b>Extended Key</b></p>

<ul>
<li>All features of Contrib Key</li>
<li><b>Cloud synchronization</b>: Data can be synced between multiple devices via WebDAV and Google Drive</li>
<li>Daily <b>automated backup</b> with optional upload to cloud storage</li>
<li>Sophisticated, customizable <b>CSV import</b> (spreadsheet applications)</li>

<p><b>Professional Key</b></p>

<ul>
<li>All features of Extended Key</li>
<li>Unlimited number of <b>templates and plans for split transactions</b></li>
<li><b>Email support</b> with guaranteed response after 2 business days</li>
<li>Voting right on <b>development roadmap</b></li>
</ul>

<p><i>My Expenses</i> requires the following <u>permissions</u>:</p>

<ul>
<li><i>Calendar</i>: Plans for future and recurrent transactions are stored either in a special local calendar or in any other calendar you assign.</li>
<li><i>Storage</i>: Storage of QIF and CSV exports and backup files.</li>
<li><i>Internet</i>: Licence key validation, Cloud synchronization.</li>
<li><i>Accounts</i>: Synchronization across devices via cloud services.</li>
</ul>
